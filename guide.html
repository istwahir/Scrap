How to Recycle with Us

Step 1: Sort Your Recyclables

Separate your recyclables into different categories:

  • Plastics (bottles, containers)
  • Paper (newspapers, cardboard)
  • Metal (cans, foil)
  • Glass (bottles, jars)
  • Electronics (phones, cables)

Step 2: Clean & Prepare

Ensure all items are:

  • Empty and rinsed
  • Free from food contamination
  • Dry and clean
  • Labels removed (if possible)
  • Crushed or flattened to save space

Step 3: Request Collection

Use our platform to:

  • Submit a collection request
  • Choose convenient pickup time
  • Add pickup location details
  • Upload photos of materials
  • Track request status

Step 4: Earn Rewards

Benefits of recycling with us:

  • Earn points for each collection
  • Track your environmental impact
  • Redeem points for rewards
  • Get special bonuses for regular recycling
  • Share achievements with friends

Plastic

Accepted Items:

  • PET bottles
  • HDPE containers
  • Clean plastic bags
  • Food containers
  • Bottle caps

Not Accepted:

  • Contaminated plastics
  • PVC pipes
  • Styrofoam
  • Medical waste

Paper

Accepted Items:

  • Newspapers
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Office paper
  • Magazines
  • Books

Not Accepted:

  • Waxed paper
  • Food-soiled paper
  • Tissues
  • Laminated paper

Metal

Accepted Items:

  • Aluminum cans
  • Steel cans
  • Metal bottle caps
  • Clean foil
  • Scrap metal

Not Accepted:

  • Paint cans
  • Aerosol cans
  • Batteries
  • Electronics

Glass

Accepted Items:

  • Clear glass bottles
  • Colored glass bottles
  • Glass jars
  • Glass containers

Not Accepted:

  • Window glass
  • Mirrors
  • Light bulbs
  • Ceramics

Electronics

Accepted Items:

  • Mobile phones
  • Computers
  • Cables
  • Small appliances
  • Batteries

Not Accepted:

  • Large appliances
  • Damaged batteries
  • Medical devices
  • Security equipment

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the collection process work?

Submit a request through our platform, and we'll match you with a nearby collector. They'll pick up your recyclables at your chosen time and location.

How do I earn points?

Points are awarded based on the type and quantity of materials recycled. You'll also earn bonus points for consistent recycling and referrals.

What can I do with my points?

Points can be redeemed for M-Pesa cash, airtime, or eco-friendly products from our partner stores.

How do I track my environmental impact?

Your dashboard shows metrics like CO‚ÇÇ reduced, trees saved, and water conserved based on your recycling activity.

What happens to my recyclables?

Materials are sorted and sent to certified recycling facilities where they're processed into new products or raw materials.

How can I become a collector?

Register as a collector through our platform. You'll need to provide identification, vehicle information, and complete our verification process.
